ProofTrace Privacy Policy

ProofTrace runs locally on your device. It scans selected files for embedded provenance markers and tool-signature indicators.

No files are uploaded. No scan content is transmitted to any server.

Local data storage

ProofTrace stores scan reports, manifests, and logs locally in your Documents folder to support auditing and export.

Analytics and tracking

ProofTrace does not use advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics.
